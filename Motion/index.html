The Beauty of Music

Beauty and curiousity are the hand maidens of the mind!

Why is music beautiful to me?

How does my mind organize the sounds into an emotioanl and intellecual response?

The interplay of musical logic and emotions in this abstract dimension has always been a mystery to me.

I have a multitude of questions with no answers.

Do I really need answers when I listen to Mozart, Cole Porter, Stravinsky and Duke Ellington?

Enjoy and marvel at the miracle of the experience.

A beauiful sunset is both a simultaneous question and answer and, truly, inseparable.

Here is a great book to read, Sound in Motion by David Mcgill.

The whole universe is in motion.

The ocean is life in motion.

Enjoy the music of Bach-The mind in motion!
